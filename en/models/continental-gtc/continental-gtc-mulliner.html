THE ULTIMATE HANDCRAFTED CAR

To drive the world’s most finely crafted luxury grand tourer is one thing. To drive it with the top down is quite another. Combining exhilarating performance with exquisite Mulliner craftsmanship and the latest automotive technology, the Continental GTC Mulliner is a convertible GT unlike any other.

Available with either a 4.0 litre V8 engine or a 6.0 litre W12 engine, it features digitally controlled chassis systems that guarantee a glorious drive, whatever the weather. With innovations in comfort, handling and convertible design, this is a car you can enjoy driving with the top down all year round. Consider it the most exquisitely crafted convertible on the road.
 

Design

STYLE WITHOUT EQUAL

Build your Continental GTC Mulliner

TURN HEADS ON ARRIVAL
Crafted to match the Mulliner detailing seen elsewhere on the car, the gleaming radiator grille is paired with bright chrome lower bumper grilles, to make an instant statement.

FIRST IMPRESSIONS COUNT
The exquisite convertible lines of the Continental GTC are complemented with exclusive Mulliner detailing, including gleaming wing vents, Mulliner badging and mirror caps painted satin silver.

PERFECTLY CRAFTED WHEELS
The 10-spoke 22” Mulliner wheels feature a duo-tone painted and polished finish that creates a sense of extra depth. In the centre, self-levelling wheel badges keep the Bentley ‘B’ logo upright at all times.

INCREDIBLE ATTENTION TO DETAIL
The Bentley interior has been crafted to create a sense of wonder, from the illuminated treadplates to the Mulliner instrument panel graphics. Emblazoned on the exclusive Grand Black Veneer fascia you will see the car’s convertible silhouette.

A CABIN WITH A UNIQUE CHARACTER
You can choose from eight seating and trim colour combinations, each one featuring a unique three-colour split. Throughout the interior, you will benefit from Mulliner embroidery that matches your chosen accent colour.

LIGHT, COLOUR AND CRAFT
Running the length of the cabin, the centre console features a Diamond Milled finish, while the waistrails feature chrome Bentley ‘B’ overlays. Adjustable mood lighting completes the picture.

Performance

Effortless Power

Enquire to Buy

Nothing beats the sheer joy of driving the world’s most finely crafted convertible GT car, whether you have the roof up or down. When fitted with the V8 engine, it can take you from zero to 60 mph in just 3.9 seconds (0 to 100 km/h in 4.0 seconds). Choose the W12 engine and that time drops to just 3.5 seconds (0 to 100 km/h in 3.6 seconds). With a suite of computer-controlled chassis systems, it delivers a truly unrivalled drive. After W12 launch

Exhilaration comes in many forms, however, which is why whichever engine you select, you can choose from four driving modes at any time: Sport for spirited driving, Comfort for relaxed cruising, Bentley for a balance between the two and Custom, for the opportunity to create your own perfect configuration. When you drive the Continental GTC Mulliner, you will always be in control. After W12 launch

Technology

A DIGITAL DREAM

  • A REFINED ATMOSPHERE

    Technology is used to transform the experience of every occupant, from the Pirelli Noise Cancellation System to the built-in air ioniser that continuously purifies the air while the roof is closed.

  • A CONNECTED CABIN

    The car features Apple CarPlay*, alongside its own Wi-Fi hotspot and wireless phone charging. My Bentley connected services include Online radio and over-the-air updates for the satnav maps. See all My Bentley connected services.

    *Apple CarPlay is a registered trademark of Apple Inc.

  • EFFORTLESS TOURING

    To make highway driving even easier, the Continental GTC Mulliner is fitted with the Touring Specification, including features such as Adaptive Cruise Control, Lane Assist, a Head-Up Display and a Night Vision Camera.

  • SAFER URBAN DRIVING

    Installed as standard, the City Specification adds a Top View Camera and warning systems that alert you to unseen vehicles or pedestrians, plus Traffic Sign Recognition* and mirrors that dim automatically when they detect glare.

  • HEARING IS BELIEVING

    For music, you can choose between three premium sound systems: the standard-issue Bentley Signature Audio, the 16-channel Bang & Olufsen for Bentley or the incredible, 20-channel Naim for Bentley.

  • SAFE AND SECURE

    The My Bentley smartphone app can alert you if your car’s alarm is activated in your absence – and if it is moved without your consent. Private eCall, meanwhile, will alert the emergency services if your car is involved in an accident.

Personalisation

YOUR BESPOKE GTC

Your journey begins with a standard palette of 16 exterior paint colours – a figure that grows to more than 80 if you consider the extended range and Mulliner Paints. The roof can be made in your choice of seven fabrics, complementing your paint choice perfectly. The optional Mulliner Blackline Specification takes all the car’s polished exterior trim and renders it in black, adding contrast and a certain edge. It also includes a black finish for the 22” Mulliner wheel.

Inside, you can choose from any Bentley veneer, plus practically any feature from Bentley’s range of optional equipment and accessories. Further ideas for personalisation are available in the Personal Commissioning Guide, available from your Bentley dealer.
