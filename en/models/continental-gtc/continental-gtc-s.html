Performance

DRIVE LIKE THE WIND

PREPARE FOR A POWER SURGE
A twin-turbocharged 4.0 litre V8 petrol engine delivers 542 bhp (550 PS) â€“ which means you can go from zero to 60 mph in 4.0 seconds (0-100 km/h in 4.1 seconds) and on to a top speed of 198 mph (318 km/h). You can open or close the roof at speeds of up to 30 mph (50 km/h).

EXPERIENCE SPORT MODE
Four driving modes mean you can set the driving characteristics to suit you. As well as Comfort, Bentley and Custom, which lets you create your own mode, there is Sport Mode, which calibrates the carâ€™s settings for a truly thrilling experience.

HEAR THAT V8 ROAR
With Sport Mode activated, the car sounds as good as it looks, thanks to the thrilling roar emitted by the Sports Exhaust. Think of it as one more reason to drive the Continental GTC S with the top down.

STABLE WHEN CORNERING AT SPEED
Bentley Dynamic Ride is an innovative 48-volt anti-roll system that automatically stiffens the roll bars when the car is cornering hard, then relaxes its grip to allow for a more refined ride at other times. Consider it the best of both worlds.

TRACTION AT ALL TIMES
Adaptive all-wheel drive means you can enjoy the agility and control provided by rear-wheel drive, when conditions allow. Yet the moment traction is compromised, the car will automatically switch to all-wheel drive, for increased grip.

OPTIMISING FUEL EFFICIENCY
The engine features variable displacement, which means that when driving in a relaxed fashion, four of the eight cylinders switch off, to reduce fuel consumption and emissions. Put your foot down in earnest and full power returns instantaneously.
