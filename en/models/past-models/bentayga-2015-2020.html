The Bentayga Range (2015 - 2020)

Bentayga is the first Bentley SUV - also the world's first true luxury SUV. Launched in 2015, Bentayga was named after the Roque Bentayga, on the island of Gran Cananria, Spain, and was designed, engineered and crafted to offer unprecedented levels of refinement and performance. Bentayga was created after consultation with Bentley owners and meticulous research to ensure this new model included the luxurious features and dynamic drive experience one expects of a Bentley. 

After launching the first Bentayga with a W12 engine came the addition of Bentayga Diesel, then the luxurious Bentayga Mulliner. These were joined by the agile Bentayga V8 and the serene Bentayga Hybrid - Bentley's first step towards electrification. The final addition to the range was Bentayga Speed, which at launch was the world's fastest SUV, and remains amongst the quickest SUVs in the world.

In 2020, Bentley has launched the new Bentayga, the second generation of this luxurious SUV. With the new Bentayga range, Bentley continues to innovate and explore the possibilities which arise where ultimate luxury meets thrilling performance.

Bentayga (2015 - 2020)

Bentayga, at its launch in 2015, was the most luxurious, hand-built, all-terrain vehicle ever made: a car in which technology, speed and power came together to offer an extraordinary driving experience. 

This modern SUV’s exterior features a muscular powerline -  the sharply-defined crease that draws your eye from the front wheel along the side of the car  It is complimented by a B-shaped wing vent, extensive brightware and large, impressive wheels which add a further level of striking detail.

The interior of this refined car is beautifully handcrafted, too. Sustainably sourced wood veneers are carefully selected, cut and matched to create a seamless ‘ring of wood’ around the driver and passengers. A panoramic sunroof allows light to reflect off the solid metal detailing with precision-knurled finish and the hand-stitched leather interior features, while optional mood lighting can be adjusted to refine the atmosphere of the cabin. 

With its W12 petrol engine, the first generation Bentayga redefined what an SUV engine could do. Its 6.0 litre engine achieves 0 to 60 mph in 4.0 seconds (0 to 100 km/h 4.1 seconds) and accelerates to a top speed of 187 mph (301 km/h).

Designed for both performance and efficiency, the technologically advanced W12 petrol engine with Variable Displacement  delivers 5-90% torque within 1.1 seconds, offering maximum torque at low engine speeds and creating an effortless driving experience.

Connected services have been introduced to the original Bentayga:  please refer to the My Bentley Support website to see which services are available for your model and region.

Bentayga Diesel (2016 - 2020)

The 2016 Bentayga Diesel was the first diesel in Bentley's history. The world’s most refined 8-cylinder diesel engine, it was developed to deliver world-class Bentley performance with the extended range of a diesel engine.  

This model features a number of distinctive and exclusive design features including a gloss black matrix grille with a chrome surround and centre bar, along with two twin-quad tailpipes at the rear. It also features a ‘V8 DIESEL’ badge that sits on the lower edge of the front doors. In addition, ‘Bentley’ treadplates and the classic ‘B’ design foot pedals are fitted as standard.

Its 4.0 litre, 8-cylinder, 32-valve diesel engine delivers a top speed of 168 mph (270 km/h) and achieves 0 to 60 mph in 4.6 seconds (0 to 100 km/h in 4.8 seconds).  With a power output of 429 bhp (435 PS), this engine delivers class-leading drivability and refinement. It will come as no surprise that it feels and responds in a familiar way – like a typical Bentley engine.

Bentayga Diesel’s extended range of over 1000km is made possible courtesy of its highly efficient engine, an 85-litre tank and a host of other technical refinements. The engine also delivers a combined fuel consumption of only 35.3 mpg (8.0 l/100 km).

Bentayga Mulliner (2017 - 2018)

Bentayga Mulliner was designed to embody the ultimate expression of the world’s most luxurious SUV.

Developed and created by Mulliner and launched in 2017, it took the Bentayga to another level of luxury through the exquisite craftsmanship for which Mulliner is renowned.

Produced in limited numbers, Bentayga Mulliner features Mulliner 22 inch Paragon wheels, LED welcome lamps and Mulliner treadplates. Inside, Bentayga Mulliner includes the four-seat Comfort Specification with rear centre console, including rear seatswhich adjust in 18 different ways, and include massage and ventilation functions.

The interior also features cabin waist rails and console finished with new Ombré Burr Walnut veneer, transitioning seamlessly from Piano Black stain in the front cabin to Burr Walnut in the rear. The Mulliner handcrafted duo-tone colour split is complemented by unique two colour accent stitching on a quilt pattern with Mulliner contrast accent colours. Mulliner seat embroidery adds a finishing touch in a contrast accent colour.

A unique addition is the Mulliner bottle cooler and chiller, fully integrated into the rear console. The cooling compartment is backlit with LED lighting and features two handcrafted Cumbria Crystal champagne flutes and an illuminated bottle chilling ring. The frosted glass soft-close door is etched with the Mulliner branding.

Bentayga V8 (2018 - 2020)

Launched in 2018, Bentayga V8 balances exquisite refinement with astonishing performance. Bold, contemporary styling cues and unparalleled levels of craftsmanship provide a sleek, sporting look, while the new cutting-edge V8 engine delivers immense power and instant acceleration. 

Bentayga V8 shares the same sleek lines as the other models in the Bentayga range, but adds some distinctive features to create an edgier exterior that matches the car’s dynamic feel. A black and chrome radiator matrix grille adds extra presence at the front, while the rear is enhanced by two twin-quad tailpipes, with a selection of large, striking wheels completing an imposing look.

The handcrafted cabin of the new Bentayga V8 features elegant and comfortable straight fluted seats  and the upper cabin is trimmed in Eliade.  Bentley treadplates and foot pedals in the classic ‘B’ design as standard provide the perfect finishing touches.

Powerful and efficient, with seamless power delivery, Bentayga V8's innovative engine, launched with the model in 2018, provides an effortlessly engaging drive – both on and off the road. It delivers an impressive 542 bhp (550 PS) of power that can take it to a top speed of 180 mph (290 km/h).  

The V8 engine achieves its maximum torque of 770 Nm (568 lb ft) at 1,960 rpm and maintains this right through to 4,500 rpm, ensuring rapid acceleration at any engine speed. Bentayga V8 accelerates from 0 to 60 mph in just 4.4 seconds (0 to 100 km/h in 4.5 seconds), contributing to the car’s agile, sporty driving experience.

Bentayga V8 Design Series
Bentayga V8 Design Series is a design-focused expression of this luxurious SUV. From the moment it was launched, it has taken the sleek, contemporary feel of Bentayga to a new level, with a selection of stylish design details that highlight its intense, sporting nature.

It includes the Blackline Specification as standard, which replaces every piece of brightware with a brooding black-painted trim. A body-coloured lower front bumper apron at the front of this refined car and twin gloss black tailpipes at the rear, a stylish chrome and enamel Design Series badge positioned low on the door create a stylish, dramatic exterior.The distinctive, contemporary look continues inside with Beluga hides and a contrast accent colour.

A carbon fibre weave was created exclusively for the Design Series, and is applied to the cabin fascias and door waistrails, and is complemented perfectly by gleaming Piano Black veneer. 

Bentayga V8 and V8 Design Series feature a range of *connected features including Driver assistance technology included as standard, including Exit Warning and a rear view camera, makes the roads easier to navigate, while Bluetooth, Wi-Fi and Apple CarPlay connectivity allows passengers to stay connected to the outside world.

*Not all features are available in all regions. Please visit the My Bentley Support website for more details.

Bentayga Hybrid (2018 - 2020)

Bentayga Hybrid was Bentley’s first step on a journey towards an electric future, embracing forward-thinking technology to enhance modern living. It, at its launch in 2018, was the world’s first luxury hybrid.

Discreet Hybrid badging on the tailgate and lower door area are accompanied by painted 21” five twin-spoke alloy wheels as standard. Bentayga Hybrid’s upper cabin is trimmed in Eliade, a contemporary technical fabric that contrasts perfectly with the lacquered veneers running around the cabin. Exquisitely comfortable fluted seats and a tactile diamond knurling finish are applied to the driver controls. The satisfying silence of the all-electric driving mode is enhanced by eight layers of noise isolation that keep the sounds of the city at bay.

Bentayga Hybrid's class-leading V6 petrol engine contains a twin-scroll turbocharger that enables rapid response and high efficiency, and the engine works seamlessly with the electric motor to provide an impressive 700 Nm of combined torque. This allows Bentayga Hybrid to go from 0 to 60 mph in 5.2 seconds (0 to 100 km/h in 5.5 seconds) and on to a top speed of 158 mph (254 km/h), making this remarkable SUV as exhilarating as it is serene.

Bentayga Hybrid has three modes - Hybrid, Hold and the all-electric EV Drive - covering between them every conceivable customer requirement. In Hybrid mode, intelligent technology ensures the most appropriate energy source is always used, helping to maximise electric driving and minimise emissions and fuel consumption. Intelligent navigation works with the car’s navigation system to preserve battery charge for urban areas. An intelligent accelerator pedal ensures you can feel when you’re about to engage the V6 engine, giving you total control, while intelligent coasting switches off the petrol engine when you take your foot off the accelerator to increase efficiency even further.

A selection of online services accessed through the My Bentley app* use seamless connectivity to enhance the hybrid experience. These include ‘My battery charge’ E-charging, ‘My cabin comfort’  and ‘My trip statistics’. 

*Not all services are available in all regions. See the My Bentley Support website for service availability.

Bentayga Speed (2019 - 2020)

Launched in 2019, Bentayga Speed was, at its launch, the world’s fastest SUV, delivering a singular balance of phenomenal power and exquisite levels of craftsmanship. With a top speed of 190 mph (306 km/h), it offers an invigorating driving experience that both thrills and captivates.

The striking styling of Bentayga Speed includes a spoiler launched for this model, coupled with the body-coloured Styling Specification and lower bodywork. Dark tint lamps, radiator shell matrix and lower bumper grilles further enhance its performance credentials, while 22” Speed Wheels, add a distinctively sporty edge.

The interior includes Mulliner Driving Specification as standard, including diamond-quilted seats, sports pedals and embroidered detailing.  Alcantara is applied to the leather on the cushion and backrest centre panels, the steering wheel, the gear lever, the lower knee rolls, the upper pillar trim and the headliner.

With a maximum speed of 190 mph (306 km/h), Bentayga Speed provides a driving experience that is truly exciting and enthralling. Featuring a class-leading W12 engine with 626 bhp (635 PS), it accelerates from 0 to 60 mph in 3.8 seconds (0 to 100 km/h in 3.9 seconds). 

Enhancements to the Sport mode offer a dynamic and sporty drive experience, forming a striking contrast to the more fluid experience of Comfort mode. 

Bentley Speed features *convenient technology like Apple CarPlay®, allowing you to control your iPhone® through your car’s touchscreen, as well as safety features like Private eCall, which alerts first responders in the event of an emergency. 

*not all features available in all regions. Visit the My Bentley Support website for further information about service availability.
