THE DEFINITIVE LUXURY SUV

Combining exhilarating performance with exquisite craftsmanship, the Bentayga is an SUV without compromise. You can drive it anywhere, from city streets to the open road and beyond. And with a choice of seating configurations including 4-seat, 5-seat and 7-seat options, you can take everyone and everything with you.

Four models are available in the Bentayga range: the quintessential Bentayga, the bold Bentayga S, the phenomenally powerful Bentayga Speed and the luxurious Bentayga Azure, packed with technology designed to boost every occupant’s wellbeing. Perfect for business, leisure and everything in between, the Bentayga is a luxury SUV like no other.

Performance

POWER ON DEMAND

Enquire to buy

There are three powertrains available in the Bentayga range, depending on which model you choose. Select the Bentayga Azure or Bentayga S and you will enjoy thrilling performance, thanks to a 4.0 litre, twin-turbocharged V8 petrol engine. With the Bentayga model you can choose between the same V8 engine or a 3.0 litre petrol hybrid. For those who want the ultimate in power, however, the decision is easy: the Bentayga Speed, built on Bentley’s phenomenal 6.0 litre W12 petrol engine.

Every Bentayga features active all-wheel drive, with all V8 and W12 models benefiting from Bentley Dynamic Ride – an innovative, electronic anti-roll system that makes adjustments automatically in response to driving conditions. It helps keep the cabin stable when cornering at speed, while also reducing vibrations and improving ride quality on the open road.

Offering extreme performance with no compromise on comfort or practicality, the Bentayga is built for every road – and for places with no roads at all.

Design

UNRIVALLED ATTENTION TO DETAIL

Build your Bentayga

The Bentayga exudes presence from the moment you see it approaching. An imposing grille – its finish dependent on your choice of model – sits atop a sculpted front bumper. The grille is flanked by LED matrix headlamps with an intricate design that mimics cut crystal, complementing the jewelled 3D lamps at the rear. In profile, gleaming details catch the eye, with badging, wing vents and side sills accentuating the car’s sleek form. Its commanding stance is emphasised by exquisitely designed wheels – 21” are standard on the Bentayga, and 22” on the Bentayga S, Bentayga Speed and Bentayga Azure.

Inside, hand-stitched leather meets hand-finished veneers, in a stunning cabin space that can be commissioned according to your taste. A luxurious four-seat configuration offers extra comfort in the rear, while the five-seat option allows the rear seats to be folded down fully. For larger families, there is also a seven-seat option.

 

Technology

A DIGITAL MASTERCLASS

  • FIRST-CLASS INFOTAINMENT

    Every Bentayga is equipped with the wired and wireless versions of Android Auto* and Apple CarPlay**, alongside a suite of connected services delivered via the My Bentley app. Media inputs include two Type-C connectivity points and digital TV. The Bentley Signature Audio system is fitted as standard, however you can opt to upgrade it to the 20-speaker Naim for Bentley system, for truly unrivalled sound quality. Learn more about My Bentley connected services.
     

  • REAR-SEAT LUXURY

    Rear-seat passengers can use the wireless Touch Screen Remote to control functions including the audio, heating, ventilation and mood lighting settings. The optional Bentley Rear Entertainment system adds two 10.1” screens in the rear, for viewing content streamed from mobile devices. Sound can be channelled through the headphones provided, the passengers’ own equipment or the car’s audio system.

  • DRIVING ASSISTANCE

    The Bentayga includes Bentley’s City Specification as standard, with the optional Touring Specification adding features designed to make long distance drives a pleasure, such as Adaptive Cruise Control, a Night Vision Camera and a Head-Up Display.

Personalisation

Commissioning your Bentayga

  • CREATE YOUR IDEAL SUV

    You can commission a Bentayga or Bentayga S in any one of seven standard colours – with the choice rising to 16 for the Bentayga Speed or Bentayga Azure. The extended range widens that choice to around 60 colours, plus duo-tone options and Mulliner Paints. The Blackline Specification turns all the brightly finished exterior details black, bar the ‘BENTLEY’ lettering and wing badges. The Styling Specification goes even further, adding a full carbon fibre body kit, for additional road presence.

     

  • START WITH THE FINISH

    Inside, you can choose from a range of traditional high-gloss wood veneers, or contemporary new open pore options that use less lacquer. Technical, non-wood options are also available, such as carbon fibre and Dark Tint Diamond Brushed Aluminium. You can even opt for the beautiful new Piano Linen by Mulliner finish on the fascia and waistrails.

    The Colour Specification gives you even more hide colours to choose from, while the opulent Leather Specifications replace the cloth-trimmed areas of the cabin with leather.

  • THIS IS JUST THE BEGINNING

    Features you can add include Picnic Tables mounted on the backs of the front seats, mood lighting and illuminated treadplates. The Front Seat Comfort Specification adds ventilation and massage functions to the front seats, alongside adjustable cushion and backrest bolsters, and winged comfort headrests. The Four Seat Comfort Specification gives you two fully featured rear seats, plus a luxurious centre console in the front and the rear of the cabin.
