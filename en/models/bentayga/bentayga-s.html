Bentayga S

The Bentayga S is an SUV with attitude, featuring bold, eye-catching design, inside and out. Pairing unique sports styling with a twin-turbocharged 4.0 litre Bentley V8 engine, this is more than just an SUV. It is a statement of intent.

MAKE YOUR MARK

The Bentayga S is designed to make heads turn and hearts skip a beat. Characterised by a suite of high-contrast details, it is a stunningly crafted SUV that is styled to stand out from the crowd. Yet as with every Bentley, there is more to it than meets the eye. With a powerful V8 engine and an array of innovative technology – including the thrilling enhanced Sport Mode – this is a vehicle for those who demand the power to make an unforgettable impression, wherever they drive.

Design

A DARKER EDGE

Build your Bentayga S

A STRIKING CONTRAST
As well as dark-tint headlamps and taillamps, the matrix grille and tailpipes are finished in black, as is all the exterior brightware, other than the BENTLEY lettering and wing badges.

ENHANCED ATTITUDE
The sleek lines of the Bentayga have been given additional visual impact, with a body kit that includes black painted side sills, front and rear bumper details, and an elongated rear tailgate spoiler.

STUNNING 22” WHEELS
Red-painted brake callipers peer through imposing 22” wheels designed exclusively for the Bentayga S, which can be commissioned in your choice of three different finishes.

INSTANTLY INDENTIFIABLE
Even when you sit inside, there is no mistaking the identity of this car. ‘S’ badging can be seen on the fascia and the treadplates, as well as unique ‘Bentayga S’ embroidery on the fluted leather seats.

A TACTILE INTERIOR
The handcrafted leather hides used to upholster the interior feature an exclusive, eye catching colour split. Lightweight, suede-style Dinamica is applied to frequently touched areas, for extra visual interest and a sure grip.

PIXEL-PERFECT DETAILS
Wherever you look, you will see Bentley’s legendary attention to detail in action – even in the digital elements of the car’s interior, where the graphics in the driver’s instrument panel reflect the car’s sporting theme.

Performance

FEEL THE POWER OF THE V8 ENGINE

Enquire to Buy

PUT YOUR FOOT DOWN
A twin-turbocharged, 4.0 litre V8 engine delivers 542 bhp and 770 Nm of torque – which can propel you from 0-60 mph in 4.4 thrilling seconds – and on to an exhilarating top speed of 180 mph.

LET THE ADRENALINE FLOW
Select the Enhanced Sports Mode and you will experience a transformation in the way it drives, as settings for the engine, transmission and chassis are optimised for maximum excitement.

TURN UP THE VOLUME
There is no mistaking the growl of a car with a V8 engine. That’s why the Bentayga S is fitted with a Sports Exhaust as standard, to make the most of its inimitable V8 roar when driven in Enhanced Sports Mode.

EFFICIENT OPERATION
Whenever the full power of the engine is not required, it can automatically shut off four of its eight cylinders, improving efficiency and reducing emissions. These cylinders re-engage automatically, the second you need them.

STAY IN CONTROL
Bentley Dynamic Ride is a powerful electronic anti-roll system that helps keep the cabin of the V8 level when cornering, without compromising on the refined ride you expect on the open road.

GO FURTHER
The optional All-Terrain Specification adds driving modes for four different low-traction surfaces, as well as underfloor protection, allowing you to drive your Bentayga S wherever you need to.

Technology

FUTURE PROOF

My Bentley connected services
include Android Auto* and Apple CarPlay**, as well as a built-in wired and wireless Wi-Fi hotspot, Online radio and Real-time traffic information. The Touchscreen Remote lets rear-seat passengers access features such as air conditioning and the music system. Driver assistance features include the optional Touring Specification, offering Adaptive Cruise Control plus a head-up display, night vision camera and Lane Assist. Together, they make long journeys easier and safer. The My Bentley smartphone app, meanwhile, includes a number of remote access features, enabling you to find your car easily and even receive an alert if it is moved without your consent.

Personalisation

YOUR MOVE

You can choose from seven standard exterior colours, a figure that rises to around 60 when you explore the extended range. Duo-tone finishes and Mulliner Paints give you even more options.

A wide choice of interior hide colours is available, as are options for stitching, piping and embroidery. Veneer options include contemporary open pore woods and technical finishes such as carbon fibre and Dark Tint Diamond Brushed Aluminium.

Further options include the Front Seat Comfort Specification, adding winged headrests, ventilation, massage functions and additional adjustments for the seat cushions and backrests. The Bentley Rear Entertainment system adds two 10.1” touchscreens to the backs of the front seats, enabling rear-seat passengers to stream content to them directly from their mobile devices.

The Engine

The Bentayga S is equipped with a twin-turbocharged 4.0 litre V8 petrol engine, for uncompromising performance.
