Bentley Network

Your personal access to the world of Bentley and beyond.

Read more

The Bentley Network is a convenient and unique app for your Apple or Android device that provides your own tailored access to Bentley, a world of digital services and an exclusive online community of Bentley owners.

Download from the App Store or Google Play Store today.

A world of Bentley at your fingertips

Alongside rich content developed exclusively for Bentley Network members, you’ll enjoy priority access to Bentley news, including the latest breaking stories from our Crewe factory.

You can discover more about our extraordinary line up of models, receive updates from your local Bentley dealer, as well as live event information, and experience longer read stories about the wider ‘World of Bentley’.

Exclusive features for Bentley owners

A range of additional features are unlocked and available exclusively for registered Bentley owners.

Successful registration unlocks access to an online community of Bentley owners on the Bentley Network, providing you a space to share your Bentley experiences, as well as thoughts and ideas with others of similar interests from across the globe.

Continue friendships created at one of Bentley’s events, or expand your network of business connections. However you choose to engage, the experience will be tailored to you.

Unprecedented access

Bentley owners can expect exclusive invitations to an incredible range of events, with priority invitations to everything from thrilling driving experiences to intimate dinners to some of the most exhilarating races in the global motorsport calendar.

The wealth of extraordinary owners services are also accessible through the Bentley Network. Initiatives include Bentley on Demand*, a service which allows customers to use a Bentley on loan in a number of locations, and Co-Creation*, which offers customers unprecedented access to Bentley’s design team to collaborate on their next Bentley commission. (*market specific)

In addition, Bentley owners also receive a digital version of the Bentley Customer Magazine within the app, allowing you to enjoy the Bentley Magazine on the go.

Just a download away

To create an account, simply download the Bentley Network app from the App Store or Google Play Store and enter your details.

Whilst available for all to download, full customer membership is strictly limited to current Bentley owners only – so during registration you will be asked for your car’s VIN number, and the name of your Bentley retailer.

Bentley individually validates every Bentley owner’s application to join, so it can take up to 48 hours before your membership is confirmed. After that, you can start to build your profile, including details of your Bentley in the ‘My Garage’ section of the app.
