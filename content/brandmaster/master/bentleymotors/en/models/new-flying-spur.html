The Flying Spur Range

In the Flying Spur, Bentley has brought together effortless performance, sculpted design and masterful technology, to create a car as graceful as it is dynamic. Whether you prefer to be the driver or the driven, consider it the definitive luxury sedan.

The Bentley Flying Spur is available with a choice of three engine configurations: Bentley’s smooth, powerful 6.0 litre W12, the agile 4.0 litre V8 or the progressive new 2.9 litre V6, teamed with a 100 kW electric motor in the new Flying Spur Hybrid*.

The Flying Spur Mulliner, meanwhile, represents the pinnacle of Bentley luxury.

To learn more, contact your local Bentley dealer.

*Flying Spur Hybrid is available to order in most markets, but is currently not available in EU27, UK, Switzerland, Israel, Ukraine, Norway, Turkey and Vietnam.

Design

Commanding form

The Flying Spur’s confident form immediately commands attention. The gloss-black radiator matrix grille with bright chrome vertical vanes contrasts the elegant curves. Sharp, uninterrupted design lines – created using the largest superformed panel in the automotive world – draw the eye over the car’s graceful yet muscular profile. By positioning the front wheels further forwards the bonnet profile has been lifted to create finely balanced proportions. The result is an exterior with both a sophisticated and attractive look.

Eye-catching, cut-crystal effect LED matrix headlamps add to the car’s assertive presence, enhanced by a chrome sleeve that allows them to sparkle like jewels, even when they’re not lit.

Available for the first time on the Flying Spur since the 1950s, a striking Flying B mascot returns to crown the matrix grille. Crafted from stainless steel and hand-polished, it houses the innovative illuminating wings which light up in synchronicity with the headlamps.

AN INSPIRING INTERIOR

Inside, breathtaking contours inspired by the Bentley wings unify the two halves of the cabin. Accentuated by a floating centre console, the wing shape rises up and flows across the dashboard, wrapping round onto the front and rear doors. This creates a sculptural flow throughout the whole cabin that enhances the feeling of spaciousness.

The console features convenient stowage compartments in both the front and rear, with wireless charging and twin USB ports in the front stowage area. Further up the console, the precision-detailed centre vent is modelled on the Bentley ‘B’ design of the rear lamps and incorporates an eye-catching Bentley clock. Available in a polished or diamond knurled finish, the design is echoed in the rear cabin’s centre vents to give the interior a harmonious feel. DESIGNED BY YOU

The exquisite design of the Bentley Flying Spur can be further enhanced, inside and out, with a number of options specially designed to elevate your journey.

The Mulliner Driving Specification offers a balanced range of elevated features and finishes for those who want to express their individuality further. A lofted diamond style has been designed for the seats, with every single diamond unique in its shape and size, as they flow gently down the seat.

Door panels can be finished in incredibly tactile three-dimensional diamond leather, or there’s the option of Mulliner three-dimensional open-pore walnut panelling. The Mulliner Driving Specification also gives you the option of a 22” wheel with directional spokes, available in a selection of finishes.

Meanwhile, an optional glass-to glass panoramic sunroof lets the light flood in, giving the space an uplifting feel. Authentic materials and artisan craft throughout the cabin create a refined and cossetting interior, with a wide selection of trim and colour options available to choose from to make the space truly yours.

Performance

POWER WITH POISE

Balance and dynamic handling have been optimised and enhanced by positioning the Flying Spur’s engine closer to the centre of the car. Meanwhile, Active All-Wheel Drive monitors the level of grip and adapts to the driving conditions and terrain to provide greater confidence at all times. With the option of switching between four driving modes, you can enjoy an engaging driving experience on demand, while remaining firmly in control.

The Flying Spur W12 harnesses the formidable nature of the 6.0 litre W12 engine, combining impressive power and torque to deliver effortless acceleration, to a top speed of top speed of 207 mph (333 km/h).

Alternatively, the Flying Spur V8 offers an exhilaratingly powerful drive – with an emotive exhaust note and responsive acceleration. It can achieve 550 PS, a top speed of 198mph (318kph), and 0-60mph in 4.0 seconds. Its light engine increasing agility and range – especially when paired with Bentley Dynamic Ride.

Either choice will deliver a spine-tingling drive, while maintaining a sophisticated and comfortable ride – with Continuous Damping Control providing a huge breadth of ride comfort and dynamic capability.

Technology

Seamless Technology

Intelligent and connected technology has been seamlessly integrated throughout the Flying Spur to provide easy and intuitive access to infotainment, navigation and environmental controls without interrupting your journey. Allowing you to be in a secluded cocoon, whilst also being in touch with the outside world should you need it.

The Digital Instrument Display gives you the information you need exactly where you need it, while the Central Display features a 12.3” HD touchscreen with refined touch sensitivity and added functionality that gives you the option to change music without losing sight of the navigation.

The stylishly designed Touch Screen Remote gives passengers in the rear of the cabin convenient access to everything you can do on the Central Display, including controlling the infotainment functions, adjusting the ventilation and opening and closing the sunroof. It can even be used to store pre-set configurations for mood lighting.

Stay smart. Stay connected

Integrated My Bentley Connected Car technology makes your journey safer, smarter and more convenient. Features include Apple CarPlay® and photorealistic landscapes through Satellite maps. Realtime Traffic Information and Swarm Technology combine to give advance warning of changing road or traffic conditions and the local hazard information. You can also access My car status – including driving range, engine diagnostics and Lock my car – through the My Bentley app, as well as safety services such as Private eCall, all designed to provide a level of convenience and assurance about your car and journey ahead.

Bentley Rotating Display

Journeys are made all the more extraordinary through innovations such as the optional Bentley Rotating Display. An element that epitomises the balance of the Bentley Flying Spur – with its three elements coming together to form something truly unique and stunning. Hidden beneath a beautifully veneered fascia, when you start the car it rotates to reveal an advanced digital display featuring apps, media and vehicle information. A further rotation reveals three classic analogue dials with jewel-like chrome bezels. So you can switch on when you need to and switch off when you want.

FOR THE CITY. FOR GRAND TOURING

The optional City Specification is designed to make your urban exploration easier, with traffic sign recognition, hands free boot opening, city assist, reversing traffic warning, top view camera and automatic dimming mirrors. Alternatively, the optional Touring Specification strengthens the Flying Spur’s ability when grand touring by providing Bentley safeguard plus, Night Vision, head up display, and adaptive cruise control.

Personalisation

Finishing Touches

Make your Flying Spur truly your own by choosing one of our 7 standard exterior paint colours, or choose an extended or Mulliner paint, including new Cambrian Grey. Explore the many colours and finishes available on the Bentley Configurator.

Bring your Flying Spur to life in a way that reflects your unique style. Two visually striking options have been designed to enhance its contemporary style: Blackline Specification replaces the exterior chrome elements with a gloss black finish, while the Carbon Fibre Body Kit creates a bold and assertive look.

Our sustainably sourced wood veneers give the fascia a remarkably individual look and feel. You can explore a choice of 11 single veneers – including new open pore Koa or Dark Burr Walnut – with or without an optional chrome pinstripe. Alternatively, you can opt for one of seven Dual Veneers by Mulliner.

The cabin is upholstered in some of the finest leather used in the automotive world, with a selection of 15 hide colours to choose from. Diamond knurling, standard on rotary switches and indicator stalks, can be expanded to the clock, along with the bullseye and centre vents, for a particularly unified feel. There is also a choice of three audio systems, including one by Bang & Olufsen and one by Naim.

Visit our configurator to explore more finishing touches and how you can create your bespoke car.
