Bentayga Azure

The Bentayga Azure elevates the luxury SUV experience, offering new levels of comfort alongside unique styling. Available with a smooth, powerful 4.0 litre V8 engine, it sets a new standard in SUV luxury.

THE LUXURY SUV REINVENTED

Luxury is one thing. But the Bentayga Azure goes much further, with a remarkable array of features designed to boost the comfort and wellbeing of everyone on board. This is an ultra-luxury SUV that takes Bentley craftsmanship to new heights, combining artisan techniques with the latest digital technology.

It can be configured with four, five or seven seats. It can transport you and your passengers in style and unprecedented comfort, through the city, on the highway or, with the optional All-Terrain Specification, beyond the road. With the City Specification and the Touring Specification as standard, driving is effortless, wherever you need to go.
 

Design

ELEVATED AESTHETICS

Build your Bentayga Azure

ELEGANCE IN MOTION
Imposing yet elegant, the Bentayga Azure exudes presence. A gleaming front grille, bright chrome lower front bumper grilles and ‘cut crystal’ LED matrix headlamps come together to create a beautifully bright front view.

EXQUISITE LINES
In profile, exquisite details reveal the car’s identity, from the prominent wing vent and sleek sills that catch the light, to the distinctive ‘Azure’ badging and even its jewel oil and fuel filler caps.

EYE-CATCHING WHEELS
The unique 22” 10-spoke Azure wheels are designed to capture attention on first sight. Available in a choice of three stunning finishes, they are exclusive to the Azure model.

SPACE AND LIGHT
Illuminated treadplates light the way as you step inside the cabin, where mood lighting is fitted as standard. Offering a choice of colours and intensities, it helps you relax, putting control of the interior ambience at your fingertips.

ARTISAN VENEERS
The cabin itself is a stunning display of automotive craftsmanship, from the leather quilting to the hand-finished veneers – which include options such as high-gloss woods, contemporary open-pore woods and technical finishes such as Dark Tint Diamond-Brushed Aluminium.

BREATHTAKING DETAILS
The closer you look, the more you see, from the exclusive Azure embroidery and the duo-tone heated steering wheel, down to the Sports Pedals and Deep Pile Overmats on the floor. Even the stitching that adorns the quilted leather can be tailored to your tastes.

Performance

TAKE FLIGHT

Enquire to Buy

The V8 powertrain can propel the Bentayga Azure from zero to 60 mph in just 4.4 seconds (0-100 km/h in 4.5 seconds) and on to a top speed of 180 mph (290 km/h). So you can expect effortless performance, whatever the driving conditions.

Bentley Dynamic Ride is an innovative anti-roll system that reduces vibration when cruising on the open road, yet keeps the car much more stable when it takes corners at speed. The result is an incredibly refined ride, contributing to the comfort of all occupants.

Technology

ENLIGHTENED INNOVATION

  • SUPREME COMFORT

    With the Front Seat Comfort Specification fitted as standard, the two front seats gain ventilation, a six-setting massage function and winged comfort headrests, plus adjustable cushion and backrest bolsters.

  • DRIVE EFFORTLESSLY

    The Touring Specification is included as standard, providing a range of driver assistance technology including Adaptive Cruise Control, Lane Detection, a head-up display and a night vision camera – perfect for smooth, stress-free journeys.

  • LET THE SHOW BEGIN

    The optional Bentley Rear Entertainment system adds two 10.1” touchscreens to the backs of the front seats, allowing passengers to stream content from their personal devices and even plug in games consoles via HDMI.

  • FINGERTIP CONTROL

    Passengers in the rear enjoy a truly luxurious experience, thanks in part to the wireless Touchscreen Remote (TSR), which can be used to control various functions, from the sound system to the heating and ventilation system.

  • ESSENTIAL SERVICES

    The car features Android Auto* and Apple CarPlay** (wired and wireless) A8so you can integrate your phone seamlessly, along with connected services including a built-in Wi-Fi hotspot and Online radio. Learn more about My Bentley connected services.

    *Android is a trademark of Google LLC
    **Apple CarPlay is a registered trademark of Apple Inc.
  • CONNECT REMOTELY

    With the My Bentley smartphone app, you can check the status of lights, locks and windows, wherever you are. You can use it to find your car if you forget where you parked – it can even alert you if the car is moved without your consent.

Personalisation

YOURS TO CREATE

Options for personalising the exterior of your Bentayga Azure include the addition of the Styling Specification – a sleek carbon fibre bodykit. The Blackline Specification can take things further, changing all the reflective exterior detailing to black. There is also the opportunity to take things to the opposite extreme, rendering all the details on the front of the car in polished chrome.

CONFIGURE YOUR CABIN
Inside, Bentley’s full range of hide colours and colour splits is included. The Leather Specification adds even more hide to the cabin, including an opulent leather lining for the cabin roof.

The Mulliner Colour Specification, meanwhile, adds eight extra colours for stitching and in-cabin accents. 

LIMITLESS OPPORTUNITY
There is a huge range of optional features available to help you create the best luxury SUV for you. They range from the 20-speaker, audiophile-quality Naim for Bentley sound system, to folding picnic tables and the 10.1” touchscreens of the Bentley Rear Entertainment system, offering even greater luxury for rear-seat passengers.
