Bentayga

The Bentayga represents a unique balance of luxurious craftsmanship, exhilarating performance and all-round versatility. Available with a choice of a 4.0 litre Bentley V8 petrol engine or a hybrid pairing a 3.0 litre petrol engine with a powerful electric motor, it is a truly remarkable SUV.

A LUXURY SUV FOR EVERY JOURNEY

Practicality is rarely the first word that springs to mind when considering a Bentley. Yet the Bentayga’s handcrafted interior can be configured with a wide range of seating options, including four, five and seven* seat layouts. So wherever you need to go and whoever you need to take with you, there is no vehicle better equipped to make every journey a joy.

With active all-wheel drive and a choice of two powertrains – a thrilling 4.0 litre Bentley V8 with a top speed of 180 mph (290 km/h) or a 3.0 litre hybrid** that can reach 158 mph (254 km/h) – it is a unique fusion of performance, craftsmanship and go-anywhere style.

*V8 only

Performance

DRIVE YOUR WAY

Enquire to Buy

When fitted with Bentley’s twin-turbocharged V8 petrol engine, the Bentayga delivers truly exhilarating performance, to the tune of a carefully calibrated V8 exhaust note. Despite its power, the engine is highly efficient for its size, thanks to features including automatic start/stop and variable displacement, which allows the engine to shut off four of the eight cylinders when its full power is not required.

With a top speed of 158 mph (247 km/h), the hybrid powertrain delivers low CO2 emissions of just 78g/km , plus the ability to drive in the serene, near-silent, electric-only EV Mode for a full 25 miles (40 km). Drive in Hybrid mode and you can benefit from Bentley’s smart navigation feature, Predictive E-Mode. It enables your Bentayga to intelligently choose the most appropriate power source at different stages of your journey, so you can preserve battery power for when you need it most, while keeping your emissions to a minimum.

Design

DETAILS MATTER

Build your Bentayga

The Bentayga brings to life the essence of Bentley, in imposing SUV form. A compelling fusion of road presence and exquisite details, it features a stunning, sculpted exterior. When viewed from the front, the famous Bentley matrix grille is flanked at either side with LED matrix headlamps, featuring a ‘crystal cut’ design.

A large wing vent, sculpted sills and bold 21” wheels enhance the car’s profile, while jewelled 3D tail-lamps adorn the rear. On the inside, meanwhile, a handcrafted interior brings together natural hides and veneers to create a truly luxurious space – yours to tailor to your tastes.

 

Technology

COMPLETELY CONNECTED

The Bentayga includes a built-in Wi-Fi hotspot as well as both Android Auto* and Apple CarPlay** – wired or wireless – enabling you to integrate the car’s infotainment system seamlessly with your phone. The wireless Touchscreen Remote gives passengers in the rear access to features such as heating and ventilation, seat settings and the audio system.

The My Bentley app offers remote access to the car, helping you locate it when parked, for example, or alerting you if it is moved without your consent. The Hybrid version adds more features, allowing you to check the battery’s charge levels and remotely heat or cool the cabin before you get in.

Personalisation

TAILORED TO YOUR TASTE

FIRST IMPRESSIONS
You can choose from seven standard exterior colours, however the optional extended range takes your choice to around 60, plus duo-tone options and Mulliner Paints. Further exterior options include 22” wheels, the Blackline Specification, which turns all exterior brightware black and the Styling Specification, which adds a carbon fibre body kit.

INNER BEAUTY
Inside, options include Seat Comfort Specification and mood lighting, while veneers can be commissioned in a choice of natural woods or technical finishes. The leather hides can be complemented with your choice of stitching, piping or custom embroidery.

SUPREME COMFORT
Choose the V8 powertrain and you can opt to add Bentley Dynamic Ride, a powerful 48-volt anti-roll system that can help keep the cabin level when cornering, while also reducing vibrations and bumps when cruising at speed. You can also add the All-Terrain Specification, which adds new driving modes for low-traction surfaces.
