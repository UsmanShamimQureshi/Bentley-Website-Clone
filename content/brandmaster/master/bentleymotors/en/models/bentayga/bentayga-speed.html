Bentayga Speed

Meet the ultimate high-performance, super-luxury SUV. With stunning Bentley craftsmanship and a phenomenal 6.0 litre, twin-turbocharged W12 engine, the Bentayga Speed delivers unrivalled power in supreme sporting style.

UNRIVALLED POWER

This is the most powerful Bentayga ever built. Capable of producing 626 bhp (635 PS) and 900 Nm (664 lb-ft) of torque, it can accelerate from zero to 60 mph in just 3.8 seconds (0 to 100 km/h in 3.9 seconds) – and on to a top speed of 190 mph (306 km/h).*

Yet the drive is only one dimension of this remarkable car. A unique Speed aesthetic is on display, inside and out. And with the level of hand-craftsmanship that only Bentley can deliver, it stands on its own as an example of luxury design. The Bentayga Speed is truly a car without parallel. Consider it the ultimate sports-focused, super-luxury SUV.

*With the optional Carbon Ceramic brakes

ENGINEERED TO EXCITE

ENHANCED SPORTS MODE
Choose between four driving modes: Comfort maximises ride refinement, while Enhanced Sports mode tunes the engine, transmission and chassis settings for unbridled exhilaration. Bentley mode offers a mid-point, while Custom lets you configure your own settings.

TURN UP THE VOLUME
Driving the Bentayga Speed is an experience that delights all the senses – and the roar of the 6.0 litre W12 engine as you accelerate is integral to the thrill of the drive. You can push this effect to the limit with the optional titanium Sports Exhaust.

COMPLETE CONTROL
The Bentley Dynamic Ride system delivers exceptional handling, keeping the cabin more stable when cornering hard, without compromising ride comfort when cruising on the highway.

GO BEYOND THE ROAD
The All-Terrain Specification is fitted as standard, offering four additional driving modes for low-traction surfaces such as snow, mud, gravel and even wet grass. It also includes added underfloor protection.

POWER WITH RESPONSIBILITY
Variable displacement technology ensures maximum engine efficiency and range between fill-ups, by deactivating six of the car’s 12 cylinders when the full power of the engine is not required.

STOP FASTER
The front wheels can be equipped with a set of 440 mm carbon ceramic disc brakes. The most powerful brakes ever fitted to a Bentley, they are among the largest in the production car world.

Design

A DARK TAKE ON BENTLEY CRAFTSMANSHIP

Build your Bentayga Speed

The Bentayga Speed is instantly recognisable, thanks to features including a body-coloured carbon fibre styling kit. Much of the exterior detailing, including the front matrix grille and Speed badging, is rendered in a dark tint finish, with the front and rear lights tinted dark to match. Red brake callipers peer through the unique 22” wheels, while the oil and fuel filler caps feature a stunning jewel finish.

Inside, there are Speed emblems on the treadplates, the fascia and the seats. A unique Speed colour split is applied to the diamond-quilted leather upholstery, which can be commissioned in any colour from Bentley’s full range. For the finishing touches, sports-style pedals complement performance-inspired graphics on the instrument panel.

Technology

FORWARD-LOOKING FEATURES

  • ASSISTANCE WHEN YOU WANT IT

    Despite its power, the Bentayga Speed is incredibly easy to drive, thanks to a suite of driver assistance features for use when driving in the city, including assisted parking and traffic sign recognition.

  • MAKE THE CONNECTION

    The car is completely connected, thanks to a built-in Wi-Fi hotspot. The wired and wireless implementations of both Android Auto* and Apple CarPlay** mean you can use your phone seamlessly with car’s infotainment system.


    *Android is a trademark of Google LLC
    **Apple CarPlay is a registered trademark of Apple Inc.

  • CONNECTED CAR SERVICES

    My Bentley connected services include Online radio and over-the-air map updates. The My Bentley smartphone app lets you connect to your car wherever you are – to locate it when parked, for example, or check the status of locks and windows.

  • AUDIOPHILE SOUND

    The car is fitted with the Bentley Signature Audio system as standard, while audiophiles can upgrade to the phenomenal Naim for Bentley system, featuring 20 speakers and a 1,720-watt amplifier, for unrivalled sound.

  • REAR-SEAT CONTROL

    Passengers in the rear of the car benefit from the Touch Screen Remote (TSR), a wireless controller that provides access to some of the most commonly used in-car functions, from heating and ventilation to the audio system.

     

  • SCREEN TIME

    The optional Bentley Rear Entertainment system adds two 10.1” touchscreens to the backs of the front seats, allowing passengers to watch content from their personal devices, plug in games consoles and even play their music on the audio system.

Personalisation

FREEDOM OF CHOICE

The Bentayga Speed can be commissioned with Bentley’s full range of interior hide colours and colour splits, as well as an equally wide range of handcrafted veneers. In addition to a wide selection of traditional high-gloss woods, there are also three open-pore wood finishes that use less lacquer for a matte effect. More contemporary still are the technical finishes on offer, ranging from carbon fibre to Dark Tint Diamond Brushed Aluminium.


In addition to the driver assistance features that come as standard, you can add the full Touring Specification, which includes features that make long journeys easier and safer, from a head-up display to a night vision camera.

 

The Engine

The Bentayga Speed is powered by a twin-turbocharged 6.0 litre W12 engine, delivering performance unlike that of any other SUV.
