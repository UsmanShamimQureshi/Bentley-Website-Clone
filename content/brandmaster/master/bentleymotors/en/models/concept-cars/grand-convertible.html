Grand Convertible Concept

When it made its debut, in November 2014, the Grand Convertible concept was described as ‘the world’s most elegant convertible’. The bespoke concept is related to the Mulsanne but is 150mm shorter, giving it a sporting character while still providing ample space for four.

One eye-catching feature is the tonneau cover, created using the largest piece of veneer ever to appear in a Bentley and finished with parallel strips of chromed steel.

This concept car showcases Bentley’s ability to create a pinnacle convertible grand tourer, while embodying luxury beyond compare.
