Flying Spur

The Flying Spur is the quintessential luxury sedan. A study in craftsmanship and contemporary design, it delivers the ultimate passenger experience. Yet looks can be deceiving. Because the Flying Spur holds a secret up its sleeve – or under its superformed bonnet, to be precise. Whether you choose the phenomenal 6.0 litre W12 engine or the dynamic 4.0 litre V8 engine, you can expect performance unlike that of any other car in its class. Because this is a driver’s car too; a place where exhilaration and refinement meet, whether you choose to sit behind the wheel or behind the front seats.

Design

Style matches substance in the most powerful Flying Spur of the range. The signature vertical vane and matrix grille combine with the perfectly balanced proportions of this four-door grand tourer to create a commanding and powerful aesthetic. On the inside, every surface is finely tuned for comfort and style, carefully crafted from the finest materials imaginable.

A CAPTIVATING EXTERIOR

With an assertive, modern design, the Bentley Flying Spur possesses an arresting road presence. Sharp lines, sculpted form and beautifully balanced proportions combine to create an elevated sense of poise and elegance.

Charisma and personality fuse thanks to carefully considered details, including a vertical vane matrix grille, cut-crystal effect headlamps, a 21” twin ten-spoke bright painted wheel as standard, and an optional  illuminated Flying B mascot. The discovery of each unique embellishment takes you on an inspiring journey.

A Harmonious Interior

The cabin of the Flying Spur is where contemporary design meets expert craftsmanship, with the result being a balance of sumptuous style and pure relaxation for both the driver and those who prefer to be driven.

The harmonious interior is sculpted from the finest possible materials and features a unique twin flute seat design – or, if you opt for the Mulliner Driving Specification, you can select the Lofted Diamond design that effortlessly combines intricate stitching and embroidery. Meanwhile, the Crown Cut Walnut veneer, with its horizontal straight-cut grain, comes as standard and contributes to the cabin’s sense of spaciousness.

POWER, UNLEASHED

The Bentley Flying Spur is built around a formidable 6.0 litre W12 engine, making for a powerful luxury grand tourer with an imposing spirit. Experience incredible power, acceleration and agile handling, with the potential to reach 0 to 60 mph in 3.7 seconds (0 to 100 km/h in 3.8 seconds) and a maximum speed of 207 mph (333 km/h). Exhilaration comes as standard, serving up an immense 635 PS of power and 900 Nm of torque.

Seamlessly master the road with rapid gearshifts and easy acceleration thanks to eight-speed dual-clutch transmission. This is a driving performance packed with poise and confidence, able to achieve maximum speeds in sixth gear, while seventh and eighth gears provide reduced fuel consumption when cruising at speed.

ABSOLUTE COMFORT AND CONTROL

Four driving modes offer comfort and control on every journey, regardless of conditions. You can choose between Comfort, Sport, Bentley and Custom, with each mode intelligently balancing the handling and power to perfection, based upon the demands of your environment. Additionally, Adaptive Valve Control even adjusts sound, allowing you to immerse yourself in a quality audible experience in Sport mode, and a more discreet exhaust note in Comfort mode.

Bentley Dynamic Ride – which comes as standard – adds even greater levels of control and confidence to your drive, with the built-in 48V Electronic Active Roll Control system designed to deliver impressive agility and stability at any speed.

Read more

A MASTER OF ALL ENVIRONMENTS

Whether you are navigating the city or crossing continents, the Flying Spur is designed for every journey.

Electronic All Wheel Steering reduces the car’s turning circle at low speeds for easier parking and manoeuvring, while out on the open road it increases the stability of the car, adding confidence when overtaking or changing lanes. With the added reassurance of fast and effective braking, along with enhanced air suspension for elevated ride comfort and impressive dynamism, you can drive with confidence at higher speeds and with ease at low speeds.

Carbon Ceramic brakes, in black or optional red, ensure that the end of your journey feels as controlled as the drive itself, with immense stopping power thanks to technology originating in the aerospace industry.

Bentley’s unique approach to blending craft and technology can also be found in the audio options available with the Flying Spur. The standard Bentley audio system delivers crisp highs, a rich bass and clear mid-tones to create the perfect soundtrack to every journey.

COLOUR AND DISTINCTION

Start your journey by choosing a colour to match your individuality. We offer a palette of 7 standard exterior colours, as well as over 80 optional extended or Mulliner paints, including new Cambrian Grey. Or, if you have a distinctive tone in mind, our Mulliner department can match any colour you desire, adding to the sense of exclusivity.

What’s on the outside is further complemented by interior colour options, with a choice of 15 stunning leather hues to add a further touch of individuality. What’s more, diamond knurling, available as standard on the main controls, can be extended to the bullseye and centre air vents as an additional touch of refinement.

A CAPTIVATING FINISH

The Bentley Flying Spur is remarkable for its carefully crafted wooden veneers, made from sustainably sourced forests and designed to further enhance a sense of balance within.

Unveil your individual interior style with a choice of 11 single veneers,including open pore veneers such as Koa, Dark Burr Walnut or Crown Cut Walnut. You also have the option to add a sleek chrome pinstripe along the centre, or dial up the decadence with one of seven Dual Veneers by Mulliner – a contrasting addition that uses a gleaming grand black veneer to make an eye-catching impact. Explore veneer options for your Flying Spur on the Bentley Configurator.

Meanwhile, a choice of 21” and 22” optional wheels in various finishes, including gloss black, allow you to tailor the character of your  Flying Spur to reflect your personality.

Explore our Configurator to create the interior of your Flying Spur.

MULLINER DRIVING SPECIFICATION

The exclusive Mulliner Driving Specification adds new levels of craftsmanship and attention to detail throughout the Flying Spur.

Three-dimensional leather door panels – inspired by the Speed 6 concept car – and a lofted diamond quilting on the seats create tactile textures and a stunning aesthetic in the cabin. Meanwhile, a larger, 22” wheel – available in various finishes – adds a formidable touch to the Flying Spur’s presence, while jewelled-finish fuel and oil caps show that no detail is overlooked when it comes to creating works of automotive art.
