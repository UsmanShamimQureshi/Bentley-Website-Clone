A GROUNDBREAKING LUXURY SEDAN

The Flying Spur range represents a combination of performance and luxury that should be impossible. For rear-seat passengers, it offers an exquisite, limousine-like experience, characterised by generous legroom, unrivalled comfort and intuitive passenger technology.For the driver, meanwhile, it delivers truly thrilling power and agility.

The Flying Spur model is the purest fusion of a high-performance grand tourer and a spacious luxury sedan. The Flying Spur Azure takes that combination further, adding a host of innovative features to boost the wellbeing of every occupant. The Flying Spur S injects extra sporting attitude,  while the Flying Spur Mulliner represents the ultimate expression of the high-performance luxury sedan.

Performance

BENTLEY HYBRID, V8 OR W12 POWER

Enquire to buy

You can choose from three advanced powertrains: a 4.0 litre V8 petrol engine, a 6.0 litre W12 petrol engine or a 2.9 litre V6 hybrid, featuring a 100 kW electric motor. Whichever you opt for, you will experience performance unlike that of any other luxury sedan.

The Active All-Wheel Drive system automatically distributes power across both axles when required, to ensure absolute confidence at all times – and to deliver the handling benefits of rear-wheel drive whenever road conditions allow.

There are four driving modes, enabling you to select a drive that suits your mood. Comfort mode optimises the drivetrain and chassis for refinement, while Sport makes the car more responsive, giving you a feeling of being more in touch with the road, rather than insulated from it. Bentley mode provides a balance of both, while Custom allows you to create your own combination of settings.

Whether you choose to be driven or to drive yourself, the Flying Spur is a car without compromise.

Design

EXQUISITE INSIDE AND OUT

Build your Flying Spur

The Flying Spur is spacious yet streamlined; imposing yet intricately detailed. Crafted from superformed aluminium for a near-seamless surface, it exudes elegance and a sense of untapped power.

From the gleaming finish of the front grilles to the matching finish of the badging and wing vents, handcrafted details can be seen everywhere on the car’s sleek exterior – while eye-catching wheels add road presence, in sizes ranging from 20” to 22”.

Inside, the Flying Spur combines a generous rear passenger space with a luxurious front cabin, for an effortless driving experience. The seats are surrounded by a ring of handcrafted surfaces – your choice from high gloss woods, contemporary open pore finishes, or technical materials such as carbon fibre and engine-turned aluminium

The leather upholstery is available in a wide range of colours, with sustainable, suede-like Dinamica accents on some models. Options for embroidery, piping and speciality stitching allow you to make the interior your own.

Technology

ALWAYS CONNECTED

The Flying Spur model range features Apple CarPlay* for smartphone connectivity. In the front, cars can be commissioned with the stunning Bentley Rotating Display, bringing together the best of Bentley’s craftsmanship and technological innovation. It allows the driver to choose between a 12.3” high-resolution touchscreen and a veneered panel housing three handcrafted analogue dials – all at the touch of a button.


Passengers in the rear benefit from the Touchscreen Remote (TSR), a removable wireless tablet that offers access to controls for the audio system, the heating and ventilation and even the massage functions in the seats.
For the driver, several assistive systems are available to make long drives easier, from Adaptive Cruise Control to Lane Assist and even a Night Vision Camera. Further features for urban driving include Park Assist and a 360º Top View Camera display.

With a choice of incredible audio systems and remote services accessible via the My Bentley app, this is a car at the cutting edge of automotive technology.
*Apple CarPlay is a registered trademark of Apple Inc.

Personalisation

A LIMITLESS OPPORTUNITY

Almost every aspect of the car can be tailored to your taste. After choosing the model and powertrain, you can choose from a generous selection of standard exterior paints, or the optional extended range. There is a wide range of wheels and exterior options – you can even opt to turn the polished detailing black.

Inside, the opportunity only grows, with a wide selection of hide colours, veneers and stitching options. To enhance the rear seat experience, the Bentley Rear Entertainment package adds removable touchscreens to the backs of the front seats, allowing passengers to stream content to them from their mobile devices.

Audio options include the standard Bentley Signature Audio, the 16-channel, 1,550 Watt Bang & Olufsen for Bentley and for the most discerning audiophiles, the 20-channel, 2,200 Watt Naim for Bentley system.
If you wish to explore the possibilities further, you can commission Bentley’s personal commissioning division, Mulliner, to create truly bespoke features for your car.
