A truly spacious luxury SUV

Prepare for the quintessential luxury SUV experience, combining the legroom of a limousine with the elevated ride height of an SUV. The New Bentayga EWB offers the perfect balance of unparalleled comfort and effortless performance. A subtle extension of 180mm delivers a radical increase in rear legroom, transforming the experience of travelling in a road vehicle.

With a wealth of innovative features for the comfort and convenience of passengers, alongside the latest technology, artisan craftsmanship and effortless power, the New Bentayga EWB is built to change the way the world sees the SUV.

*Bentayga EWB is available to order in most markets, but is currently not available in EU27, UK, Switzerland, Israel, Ukraine, Norway, Turkey and Vietnam.

Design

Space, Light and Detail

Build your New Bentayga EWB

Wherever you look, you will see the attention to detail you expect from Bentley – from the gleaming, vertical vaned grille to the contrast-coloured accents on the front and rear bumpers.

The 180mm wheelbase extension works perfectly with the Bentayga’s sleek proportions. In the rear of the cabin, where that extension translates directly into extra legroom, a 4+1 seating configuration combines a luxurious experience with the opportunity to seat a fifth occupant, if necessary.

The fluted leather seats can be commissioned in a wide range of colours, with a range of two-tone colour splits also available. Contrast stitching is available as an option, as are deep pile overmats – an especially opulent way to protect the cabin floor.

For the comfort of rear-seat passengers, the new front console includes wireless charging for smartphones and a more easily accessible touchscreen remote – the wireless device used to control many of the interior features.

Technology

Delighting the senses

  • FRONT SEAT COMFORT

    Those travelling in the front will benefit from the Front Seat Comfort Specification. It provides heated and ventilated front seats with massage functions, 20-way electrical adjustment, two memory settings per seat and comfort headrests.

  • REAR SEAT LUXURY

    The optional Bentley Rear Entertainment system adds two 10.1” screens, to which passengers can stream content from their phones. Sound can be played through the headphones included, passengers’ own equipment or even the car’s audio system.

  • THE PERFECT ATMOSPHERE

    From the audiophile-quality Naim for Bentley sound system, to beautiful waterfall lighting and zoned heating and ventilation, the in-cabin ambience has been calibrated to delight every sense, especially for passengers in the rear.

     

Personalisation

A MATTER OF TASTE

Your New Bentayga EWB can be ordered in your choice of seven standard colours, while access to Bentley’s Extended Paint Range makes around 60 colours available.

Wheels include a 21” design, which can be commissioned in a painted, machined or black painted finish. A 22” 5-spoke wheel is also available as a cost option.

Inside, a wide range of veneers is available, including three contemporary open-pore veneers and high-gloss woods, which can be further enhanced with gleaming metal overlays. Further finishes include Piano Linen by Mulliner and Dark Tint Diamond Brushed Aluminium.

Unique to the New Bentayga EWB is the optional Bentley Diamond Illumination, which allows LED lighting to shine through the upholstery itself.

Performance

DESIGNED TO BE DRIVEN

Enquire to Buy

This is a car for drivers as well as those who prefer to be driven. Its 4.0 litre V8 petrol engine delivers 550 PS and 770 Nm of torque – enough to propel the vehicle from 0 to 60 mph in just 4.5 seconds (0-100 km/h in 4.6 seconds).

With the Bentley Dynamic Ride anti-roll system to improve cabin stability and refinement on all roads, plus All-Wheel Steering for added manoeuvrability and comfort, the New Bentayga EWB is engineered for an unrivalled drive.

The Engines

The New Bentayga EWB is equipped with a 4.0 litre V8 petrol engine, for smooth, effortless acceleration.
