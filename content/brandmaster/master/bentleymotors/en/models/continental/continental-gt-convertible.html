Continental GT Convertible

Design

Truly extraordinary design

Sharing the same breathtaking styling of the Continental GT, the Continental GT Convertible is truly extraordinary. Every inch a grand tourer, it features a sleek Bentley design language, characterised by sharp lines sweeping the length of the car.

Low, wide proportions lend the car an air of power and presence – an effect emphasised by every detail, from cut crystal-effect LED matrix headlamps to LED tail lamps that resemble illuminated red gemstones.

With the four-layer insulated roof closed, the Continental GT Convertible exudes sophistication. A choice of fabrics allows you to put your personal stamp on your car – you can even opt for a stunning new Tweed roof, for a truly individual touch. All this before you even step inside.

Read more

Streamlined roof design that delights

A car of this calibre must be remarkably streamlined – not just in order to cut through the air but also to delight the eye. Which is why Bentley’s designers have gone to great lengths to ensure that when the roof lowers, the exterior and interior blend effortlessly together.

With a quick-closing mechanism, the roof folds and rests incredibly low against the car’s waistline. This allows a graceful design line to sweep up from the side panels and around the rear seats, in a single, flowing curve.

Read more

A luxurious handcrafted interior

Inside, a continuous ring of handcrafted veneer sweeps from the fascia into the doors, encircling the driver and front passenger. Design cues pick up on the exterior lines, for seamless style when the roof is down. From dual veneers and diamond knurling to the stunning Bentley Rotating Display, the interior of the Continental GT Convertible represents the ultimate in British automotive craftsmanship.

Innovative performance

The Continental GT Convertible epitomises the spirit of grand touring – the desire to drive the world’s finest roads in comfort, exhilaration and style. That’s why the 6.0 litre Bentley 12-cylinder engine beneath its bonnet delivers the perfect fusion of power and refinement. With an 8-speed dual-clutch transmission for smooth, fast gear changes, it can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in 3.7 seconds (0 to 100 km/h in 3.8 seconds) and reach a top speed of 207 mph (333 km/h). Whilst travelling at speeds of up to 30 mph (50 km/h), the roof can still be open or closed.

Learn more about the performance of the Continental GT. 

The Continental GT Convertible features a choice of four drive modes, allowing you to adjust the car’s performance to suit your journey with a simple, smooth turn of a dial. Choose from Comfort, Sport, Bentley mode or Custom, where you can configure your own combination of settings. With such a wide range of driving modes at your fingertips, you can enhance your drive with every turn.

Technology

The most advanced Continental ever

The Continental GT Convertible includes a wealth of innovative technology developed specifically to enhance the open-top experience.

While the Continental GT Convertible is built to make the most of the sunshine, it has also been engineered to excel in more wintry conditions. With Bentley’s Active All-Wheel Drive at your disposal, this is a grand tourer to be enjoyed all year round.

To complement the exhilaration of driving with the wind in your hair, both the front seats are equipped with innovative cooling technology to keep you comfortable, even in the height of summer.

On a crisp, bright day in winter, you can still enjoy the thrill of open-top driving, thanks to heated armrests, heated steering wheel and discreet front seat neck warmers that gently circulate warm air around your neck and shoulders.

Read more

The connected convertible

The Continental GT Convertible has been designed to connect seamlessly with the rest of your life. Accessed via the central infotainment screen or the steering wheel*, its digital features include Apple CarPlay®*, Satellite maps and an in-car Wi-Fi hotspot. The My Bentley app, meanwhile, gives you digital access to your car via your phone, with features such as Lock my car and My car status, which lets you check information such as fuel levels remotely.

Personalisation

Practically limitless customisation

Like every car built at Bentley’s factory in England, the Continental GT Convertible is crafted by hand. This gives you the freedom to personalise your car, from the exterior finish to the woods, hides, stitching and diamond knurled metal inside. You can even opt for a centre console finished in Côtes de Genève, inspired by the design of Swiss watches.

See how you can personalise your Continental GT Convertible here.

A wide range of exterior paint finishes are available, alongside the option to create your own personal colour with the Mulliner Paint service. Mulliner is Bentley’s personal commissioning department, responsible for bespoke customisation requests.

 

Read more

Roof options

After the exterior paintwork, the most prominent feature of a convertible is often the roof itself. That’s why Bentley gives you the opportunity to choose from a range of seven different roof finishes, including a contemporary Tweed option.

A further eight options are available for the roof headliner – the innermost layer in a four-layer insulating roof that’s so secure when closed, it can be easy for occupants to forget they’re travelling in a convertible.

Read more

Introducing Cricket Ball hide

Among the wide range of hide colours to choose from – including duo-tone themes – is a colour created specifically for the Continental GT Convertible. Cricket Ball is a deep, rich red, evocative of the leather that encases cork cricket balls. It works particularly well with the new Tweed roof.

To learn more about the options for personalising your car, please contact your preferred Bentley dealer.
