Continental GT Mulliner Convertible

Renowned throughout the automotive world for crafting truly exceptional refined and individual Bentley vehicles, Bentley Mulliner’s latest creation harnesses the beauty, power and spirit of the Continental GT Convertible to form the ultra-luxurious expression of this elegant grand tourer. Meet the Continental GT Mulliner Convertible.

This rare and exclusive car elevates the Continental to an even higher level. Crafted and curated to the smallest detail by our experts in Crewe, its thoughtful design provides you with the ability to tailor its features to fit your own style and vision.

Design

A CELEBRATION OF MODERN CRAFTMANSHIP

Every element of the Continental GT Mulliner Convertible has been considered, allowing you to appreciate every moment spent within the cockpit. Take the ornate finish of the Breitling for Mulliner Clock Face – an exclusive brushed silver timepiece with a chrome bezel surround that complements the new Diamond Milled Technical Finish of the centre console, creating a contemporary and intricate pattern that is both sleek and striking. These features are unique to the Continental GT Mulliner a statement of its coveted individuality and approach to modern luxury that can become yours forever.

The worlds of digital and analogue combine within the driver’s instrument panel, featuring newly styled dials that reflect the traditional design of the Breitling timepiece, set against the modern backdrop of the digital dashboard. Emblazoned on the Grand Black Veneer is a striking convertible silhouette denoting its Mulliner status. This stunning veneer comes as standard but can of course be tailored to your taste through Bentley Mulliner Personal Commissioning, allowing you to colour-match a Piano Veneer to your chosen interior hide or exterior paint selection. The Imperial Blue Veneer, for example, complements the open-air luxury of the convertible, hinting towards the stunning blues of the sea and sky, allowing you to feel closer to nature.

An exquisite interior

From the softest leathers and hides to a series of eight unique 3-colour seating and trim combinations, the interior of the Continental GT Mulliner Convertible can be personalised to reflect your unique style and taste. Each of the 3-colour splits includes a main hide, a secondary hide and an accent hide with a contrast and accent stitch, creating a new dimension and added character that only Bentley Mulliner can so beautifully achieve.

The eight colourways are inspired by the world around us, from high fashion to the dramatic coastal landscapes of Europe. The enchanting Amalfi colourway, for example, captures the essence of the Italian sea, cliffs and architecture, featuring a combination of Imperial Blue, Linen and Hotspur hides for a dramatic aesthetic. Equally, the stunning shades within the Flare colourway provide a burst of colour, intensified by the main hide of Hotspur, followed by Beluga and Silver accents. The remaining six colourways include Ascot, Sterling, Pavilion, Sirius, Sienna and Cambric which you can explore in more detail using our online configurator.

Stunning and immersive mood lighting within the cabin also comes as standard on the Continental GT Mulliner Convertible, allowing you to control the ambiance inside the car to reflect your state of mind. From the subtle to the vibrant, there are 14 lighting options in total, creating a space so personal, you’ll feel at home wherever your journey takes you. In addition, LED welcome lamps beam the Bentley Wings symbol to the ground as you open the door and step inside. A detail that once again denotes the thoughtful design of this immaculate car, and welcomes you like no other.

A DISCERNING EXTERIOR

Open air driving takes on a new persona in this rare grand tourer. As well as the sleek silhouette you’ve come to expect, the Continental GT Mulliner Convertible roof takes craftmanship one step further. Exclusive to this vehicle is the new, hand-stitched Tonneau cover quilting, a luxurious and intricate pattern which embraces both contrast and accent stitching to create the distinctive Bentley diamond-in-diamond quilting design concept, elevating the aesthetic with a tactile finish you can see and feel.

This unique design concept is replicated on the Mulliner branded wing vents and front grille of the car, creating a harmonious profile and powerful road presence. On the front grille, the chrome inner diamond and gloss black outer diamond creates an eye-catching visual with real depth. Further touches to the exterior profile denote this instantly recognisable car, with Satin Silver Mirror Caps providing a two-tone aesthetic that heightens its muscular and urbane appearance, while the fluid lines of the body create a streamlined form that reinforces its power and agility, ready for you to unleash.

Elevating the presence of the car further are the all-new, painted and polished 10-spoke 22” Mulliner Wheels, evoking a sense of phenomenal authority. As the wheels rotate, self-levelling Bentley ‘B’ wheel badges ensure the car’s elegance and poise remains uninterrupted, while the duo-tone finish creates depth and contours that set this car apart from anything else on the road, even when travelling at speed. Performance

An exhilarating V8 Engine

The freedom and exhilaration of open-air driving is complemented by the emotive sound of the engine burble from beneath the bonnet. With a dynamic V8 engine, the Continental GT Mulliner Convertible delivers effortless power in any condition.

The V8 engine is capable of 0 to 60 mph in 4.0 seconds (0 to 100 km/h in 4.1 seconds) before powering on to a top speed of 198 mph (318 km/h). Learn more about the GT V8 Convertible here.

Bentley Dynamic Ride

Bentley Dynamic Ride is a standard feature on the Continental GT Mulliner Convertible. This technically advanced, adaptive chassis uses Bentley’s intelligent 48-volt Dynamic Ride System to ensure a responsive ride, exceptional handling and refinement in all conditions. The system controls and adjusts the anti-roll bar of each axle to improve handling and ride comfort, as well as enhancing your driving experience to feel lighter and more precise.

Optional Carbon Ceramic brakes are available for impressive stopping power, using technology from the aerospace industry.

Mulliner Blackline Specification

Created for the Continental GT Mulliner Convertible, this exclusive specification adds a bold touch to the exterior of your car, by rendering all the chrome detailing in black. On a light or bright coloured car, the effect is increased contrast – a style guaranteed to turn heads. Apply the same treatment to a darker coloured car and the result is a sense of extra gravitas – you might even call it stealth.

The lower bumper grilles and the radiator grille bezel turn black, with the grille itself retaining its bright edging. Look at the car in profile and you will see black, Mulliner branded wing vents. Above them are door mirrors with a striking two-tone finish: the lower half finished in the same colour as the body and the top painted in Beluga gloss black. Even the edging around the hood’s tonneau cover is black.

Perhaps the most eye-catching features, however, are the gloss black 22” Mulliner wheels. Available only for Continental GT Mulliner models, they include self-levelling Bentley centre badges, each one adorned with a bright chrome ring.

Whatever your choices when you commission your car, it all adds up to a darkly cool alternative to the stunning Mulliner factory finish.
