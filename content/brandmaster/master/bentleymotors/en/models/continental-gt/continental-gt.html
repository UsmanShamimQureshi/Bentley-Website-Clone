A CONTEMPORARY CLASSIC

The Continental GT captures the essence of the Continental GT range. The quintessential luxury grand tourer, it is a true design classic, showcasing the very best of British automotive craft. There is no more accomplished combination of high performance with the practicality you need to truly enjoy long journeys.

Its twin-turbocharged 4.0 litre engine has the power to deliver exhilarating performance the moment you put your foot down, accompanied by the much-loved exhaust note that only a V8 engine can provide. With four driving modes ranging from Comfort to Sport – and the facility to configure your own settings – the Continental GT guarantees a gratifying drive.

COMMANDING PERFORMANCE

The power of the Continental GT is truly formidable: 542 bhp (550 PS), 0-60 in 3.9 seconds (0-100 km/h in 4.0 seconds) and an incredible top speed of 198 mph (318 km/h). Yet power is just one dimension of this car’s driving experience.

DRIVE IT YOUR WAY

Put the car into Sport mode and you will enjoy an instantly responsive, exhilarating drive, optimised for accelerating sharply and cornering hard. Comfort mode, on the other hand, makes the most of the car’s air suspension, offering a truly refined ride. Bentley mode provides a balance between the two, while Custom allows you to adjust individual settings, creating the driving mode that’s right for you.

ENHANCED CABIN STABILITY

The optional Bentley Dynamic Ride anti-roll system boosts ride comfort even further, allowing for tight control when pushing the car to its limits, without compromising on the smoothness you expect from a luxury grand tourer.


Design

beauty in detail

Build your Continental GT

The exterior is characterised by handcrafted details, including the Bentley matrix grille and elliptical quad tailpipes, while 21" Five Tri-Spoke painted wheels are fitted as standard. Choose the optional Blackline specification and many of these polished chrome details can be rendered in black.

Inside, a ring of Crown Cut Walnut veneer emerges from the wide centre console and flows into the doors, encircling the cabin. You can choose from five hide colours for the Bentley Continental GT interior, including deep Beluga black and Cricket Ball, a rich, warm red – all of which can be complemented with embroidery, piping, or contrast stitching. You can also choose from a wide range of optional veneers, including high-gloss and open-pore wood, plus technical materials.

Technology

Delighting the senses

  • SEAMLESS INTEGRATION

    The Continental GT features Apple CarPlay*, making it easy to integrate the infotainment system with your phone. Wireless charging enables you to charge compatible phones at the same time, while a built-in Wi-Fi hotspot connects the car to the internet whenever signal allows.

    *Apple CarPlay is a registered trademark of Apple Inc.

  • MY BENTLEY CONNECTED SERVICES

    Connected services include Online radio, over-the-air map updates for the satnav and Traffic sign recognition. The My Bentley smartphone app adds features such as Perimeter alert*, informing you if your car leaves a predefined area, alongside the facility to pre-heat the cabin to an optimum temperature.

    See the full range of services.

    * North America only
    ** EU only, requires optional Park Heater to be fitted

  • INTELLIGENT DRIVER ASSISTANCE

    Installed as standard, the City Specification includes a 360º top view camera and Traffic sign recognition*, while the optional Touring Specification covers highway driving, with features including a head-up display, night vision camera, adaptive cruise control and Lane Assist.

    * Not available in all markets

Personalisation

MAKE IT UNIQUE

The opportunity to customise your car to your tastes is virtually limitless. There are seven standard exterior colours, but if you consider the optional extended range and Mulliner Paints, your choice grows to more than 80. The Blackline Specification can give your choice a darker edge, by turning most of the exterior metal detailing black.

A wide range of wheels are available, many of which can be combined with Carbon Ceramic Brakes , featuring red or black callipers. Other exterior options include LED Welcome Lamps and a panoramic sunroof, while the Styling Specification adds a stunning Carbon Fibre body kit.

Inside, you can choose from a wide range of hides, stitching styles, piping and embroidery, while the optional Front Seat Comfort Specification adds moving headrests, adjustable cushions and side bolsters, plus ventilation and massage functions. In the Continental GT, the only limit is your imagination.
