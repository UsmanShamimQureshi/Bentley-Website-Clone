Flying Spur S

Experience a bolder, more eye-catching take on the luxury sedan. With a choice of either a 4.0 litre V8 petrol engine or a 2.9 litre V6 petrol hybrid, the Flying Spur S combines striking sports design with thrilling performance.

STYLE MEETS SUBSTANCE

The Flying Spur S takes the unmistakable presence of the Flying Spur and imbues it with a darker edge. Black exterior detailing replaces chrome, from the badges to the wheels, while an optional exterior body kit can inject even more racing flair. But its looks are only the beginning. Available with a choice of two powerful Bentley engine options – 4.0 litre V8 petrol or 2.9 litre V6 hybrid – it features a thrilling Sport mode, for a sensational driving experience. Like every car in this range, the Flying Spur S is an exceptionally spacious and luxurious four-door sedan. Yet it is also a car designed to stand out from the crowd.

Design

MAKE YOUR PRESENCE FELT

Build your Flying Spur S

TURN UP THE CONTRAST
With black gloss exterior detailing including the vertical-vaned front grille, the door mirrors and the bezels that surround the windows and the lights, the Flying Spur S offers a bolder, more purposeful vision of the four-door luxury sedan.

A CONFIDENT STANCE
The car has true road presence , thanks in part to its 21" Five Tri-Spoke wheels, exclusive to Bentley’s S models. They combine black gloss and bright machined panels, resulting in a truly striking look, whether stationary or in motion.

DETAIL DEFINED
Almost every exterior detail is infused with uncompromising attitude, from the dark tint applied to the headlamps and tail lamps to the gloss black finishers on each of the four oval tailpipes.

SIGNATURE STYLING
As you step into the car, you will see ‘S’ emblems displayed on the interior treadplates beneath each door. The exclusive theme continues throughout the cabin, with badges emblazoned on the fascia and embroidered on the fluted leather seats.

EXCLUSIVE INTERIOR DESIGN
The Flying Spur S features an exclusive upholstery pattern. Its 4+1 seating configuration displays a striking combination of primary and secondary hides that reflect the car’s sporting character.

DINAMICA DETAILS
Tactile Dinamica panels – part of the car’s exclusive upholstery design – can be seen throughout the interior, including panels on the seats, the dash, the gear lever and the heated steering wheel.

Technology

THE SMARTER SEDAN

Installed as standard, the City Specification includes a 360-degree top view camera, alert systems to make reversing safer, auto-dimming mirrors and hands-free boot opening. The optional Touring Specification adds features such as a night vision camera, a head-up display, adaptive cruise control and Lane Assist.

A suite of connected technology includes a built-in Wi-Fi hotspot, wireless phone charging and wireless Apple CarPlay**. With the My Bentley smartphone app, meanwhile, you can check the status of your car remotely. It can even alert you if the alarm goes off* or your car moves from a predefined area*.

Performance

LEAVE CONVENTION BEHIND

Enquire to Buy

POWER TO SPARE
The V8 engine delivers 542 bhp (550 PS), which results in acceleration from zero to 60 mph in just 4.0 seconds (0-100 km/h in 4.1 seconds) and a top speed of 198 mph (318 km/h). The hybrid produces 536 bhp (544 PS), taking you from 0-60 mph in 4.1 seconds (0-100 km/h in 4.3 seconds).

SPORT MODE AND BEYOND
There are four driving modes to choose from. Alongside Comfort, Bentley and Custom, they include Sport Mode, which optimises the engine, suspension and transmission settings for the most thrilling drive possible.

HEARING IS BELIEVING
When the car is driven in Sport mode, the Sports Exhaust comes into its own. Designed to maximise the roar of the engine as you accelerate, it ensures that the Flying Spur S sounds as good as it looks.

STABILITY AT SPEED
Choose the V8 configuration and you will benefit from Bentley Dynamic Ride, the 48-volt electronic anti-roll system. By tightening up when the car is cornering hard and relaxing its grip when driving in a straight line, it offers the best of both world: stability and a refined ride.

TRACTION YOU CAN RELY ON
With active all-wheel drive as standard, the car gives you the control and agility of a rear-wheel drive car – but the moment a wheel slips, it redistributes the engine’s power across both axles, which means better grip and greater confidence.

ELECTRIFYING PERFORMANCE
Choose the hybrid and you can drive on electric power alone for up to 25 miles (40 km). And with a maximum speed in EV mode of 87 mph (140 km/h) the hybrid Flying Spur S has more than enough electric power to drive at highway speeds.

Personalisation

A SINGULAR VISION

You can choose an exterior colour from a palette of standard paints and a huge number of optional colours – or go further and enlist Mulliner to create a bespoke shade, based on a sample you provide. You can even change the chrome detailing black – including the 22” wheels.

Inside, there are eight colourways to choose from, each comprising three different colours. And as alternatives to the standard Grand Black Veneer, there are many more options including high-gloss woods, open-pore woods and technical materials.

All this is just the beginning, however. To go even further, talk to your dealer about further choice from Mulliner’s Personal Commissioning Guide.
