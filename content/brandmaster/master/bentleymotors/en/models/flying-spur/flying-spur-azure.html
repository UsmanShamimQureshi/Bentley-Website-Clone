A NEW KIND OF BENTLEY FLYING SPUR

The Flying Spur Azure brings together Bentley’s exquisite hand craftsmanship with innovations in wellbeing technology to create a space that every occupant – driver and passenger alike – will enjoy immensely.

For the driver, it delivers effortless power and handling – whether you opt for laid-back luxury cruising or instead, explore its limits. This is a car that combines supreme luxury with supercar performance. For passengers in the rear, the car offers a blissful travelling experience, in a space where technology comes together with traditional craftsmanship to take comfort to the next level.

Elegant and timeless, the Flying Spur Azure sets new a new standard for luxury cars.

Design

ELEGANCE REINVENTED

Build your Flying Spur Azure

STUNNING EXTERIOR DETAILS
Gleaming Azure badges complement an elegant front grille and bright chrome lower bumper grilles. And the closer you look, the more you see – even the fuel and oil filler caps feature a stunning jewelled finish.

A SCULPTED SURFACE
Despite the generous space on offer inside, the exterior is perfectly proportioned from every angle, with a near-seamless surface achieved using sleek, superformed aluminium panels.

EXCLUSIVE WHEELS
Unique 22” wheels are available exclusively on the Flying Spur Azure. It can also be ordered with other wheels from the Flying Spur range, in sizes starting at 20”.

UNMISTAKABLY AZURE
From the Azure treadplates to the badges on the fascia and the Azure embroidery on the seats, there are countless elegant design features, many of which are exclusive to the Flying Spur Azure.

HAND-FINISHED VENEERS
The veneered fascia emerges from the centre console in a sweeping, fluid movement, inspired by the wings of the Bentley badge. From the fascia, the veneers flow elegantly into the front doors.

ELEGANCE IN DETAIL
Exquisite interior details include contrast stitching and sustainable wool trim *, lofted diamond quilting to the seats and unique three-dimensional diamond leather door trims .
*Flying Spur Azure Odyssean Edition only.

UNRIVALLED AMBIENCE
With a panoramic sunroof and extremely generous rear legroom, light and space are as important to the experience of sitting in the cabin as the handcrafted natural materials used to create it.

Performance

AN EFFORTLESS EXPERIENCE

Enquire to Buy

The V8 can take you from zero to 60 mph in 4.0 seconds (0-100 km/h in 4.1 seconds) and on to 198 mph (318 km/h). With the Bentley Dynamic Ride anti-roll system activated, the electrically powered rollbars will automatically tighten when cornering, to maintain stability without compromising ride comfort at any time.

The Bentley hybrid powertrain can propel the car from 0-60 mph in 4.1 seconds (0-100 km/h in 4.3 seconds). It can drive serenely on electric power alone for up to 25 miles (40 km), at speeds of up to 87 mph (140 km/h), making it virtually unique among luxury hybrid cars.

Technology

INNOVATIONS IN WELLBEING

  • UNRIVALLED AMBIENCE

    Technology is used to enhance the comfort and wellbeing of everyone in the car, from air ionisers that keep the atmosphere pure to mood lighting that can be adjusted for colour and intensity, according to your needs.

  • A DIGITAL DREAM

    With Apple CarPlay* for smartphone connectivity, a Wi-Fi hotspot and wireless phone charging, this is a completely connected car. Exclusive Azure graphics with an elegant bias can be seen in the digital instrument panel.

    *Apple CarPlay is a registered trademark of Apple Inc.

  • EXTREME COMFORT

    Installed as standard, the Seat Comfort Specification makes long journeys easier than ever, for both driver and passengers. It provides heating, ventilation, massage, comfort headrests, 22-way electrical adjustment for the front seats and 14-way for the rear.

  • DRIVER ASSISTANCE

    Long journeys are effortless, thanks to the Touring Specification, which includes Adaptive Cruise Control, Lane Assist, Night Vision and a Head-Up Display. The City Specification – also installed as standard – includes a Top View Camera, Traffic sign recognition* and more.

    *Not available in all markets

  • REMOTE ACCESS

    With the My Bentley smartphone app, you can access your car remotely. You can check locks, lights and fuel levels. You can also be alerted if the alarm is activated, or if your car is moved without your permission.

  • YOUR SAFETY FIRST

    The car includes Private eCall*, which can alert the Bentley Emergency Call Centre the moment the airbags are deployed. It can also be operated manually via the SOS button above the driver. Learn more about My Bentley connected services

    *Not available in all markets

Personalisation

CONFIGURE YOUR PERFECT CAR

As with any Bentley, the opportunity to personalise the Flying Spur is enormous.

There are numerous wheels to choose from, which can be accompanied by red or black brake callipers. For a darker edge, the Blackline Specification takes all the brightware – the polished metal detailing on the outside of the car – and turns it black.

Inside, select the Colour Specification and the colours of the hides and stitching are yours to choose. Veneer options include high gloss woods, dual veneers that combine panels of two different wood finishes, separated by a thin pinstripe of chrome, and other woods treated with a more minimalist open-pore effect.

There is a wealth of optional interior equipment on offer, not least a choice of three different audio systems. Bentley Signature Audio is fitted as standard, with the 16-speaker Bang & Olufsen for Bentley and the 20-speaker Naim for Bentley available as options.

Even the chassis technology is subject to your preferences, with Bentley Dynamic Ride available for the V8. An innovative electric anti-roll system, it helps the car maintain stability when cornering, without compromising ride comfort when travelling on straight roads.
